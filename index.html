About Me

Bridging AI Innovation with Real-World Impact

I'm a third-year Data Science & AI student at Breda University of Applied Sciences with a passion for transforming complex problems into elegant solutions. Over the past two years, I've worked with diverse clients including NPEC, NAC Breda, ANWB, and the Content Intelligence Agency, delivering everything from computer vision systems and NLP pipelines to production-ready MLOps deployments.

My journey spans from analyzing climate change impacts on food security to building robotic control systems for plant research, always with a focus on ethical AI and real business value. I thrive at the intersection of technical innovation and practical application, whether that's deploying models to Azure, fine-tuning transformers, or translating data insights for stakeholders.

Outside the world of algorithms and data, you'll find me behind the bar or serving tables at a local restaurant, where I've honed my communication skills and ability to thrive under pressure. When I'm not working, I'm either pushing my limits at the gym, playing field hockey with my team, or exploring new ways to balance my technical pursuits with an active lifestyle. This diverse experience has taught me that the best solutions come from understanding both the code and the people it serves.
